What is Curvi?

Curvi is a web-based application and a service that aims to help and make the process of filtering resumes or CV quickly, and allows employers to quickly search through applications to find the right person for the job.

Curvi accomplishes this by classifying applications based on their content. Then, the employer can simply search the position that they are looking for in the search bar. The applications will then be filtered so that only the ones that are relevant to the position are shown, and are sorted from the highest-scoring, or "most-skilled" applicant based on the contents of their resume.
